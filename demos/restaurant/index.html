Our Story

Born in Thessaloniki.
Alive in Every Bite.

Since 2012, Gyros Thessaloniki has carried the fire of northern Greek cooking to Bucharest. Our recipes haven't changed — they don't need to. Slow-roasted pork, hand-rolled pita, barrel-aged feta, and tzatziki made fresh every morning.

We partner with local and Greek producers, delivered daily. No shortcuts. No compromise. Just the real thing — served the way it was meant to be.
