Our Story

Born in Thessaloniki.
Brought to Your Table.

Since 2012, Gyros Thessaloniki has brought the soul of authentic Greek street food to Bucharest. Our recipes are rooted in the vibrant markets and family kitchens of northern Greece — unchanged, unapologetic, and full of character.

We work exclusively with local and Greek producers to source the freshest ingredients. From hand-cut pita to slow-roasted lamb, every element on your plate is prepared in-house with care and intention.
